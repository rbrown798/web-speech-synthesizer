Formant Speech Synthesis

Formant synthesis is a method of audio synthesis that models the resonances of a sound. Although it can be used as a technique of more general spectral modeling, it is most notable for it's ability to synthesize human speech. This speech synthesizer was made using the Web Audio API. It consists of an oscillator and a noise generator, both of which are fed through a series of resonant bandpass filters in a parallel configuration.

ARPABET

ARPABET is a system of representing English phonemes with letters, similar to the International Phonetic Alphabet.
